Hello, my name is Jenny Morgan Ryan. I am an avid learner and passionate coding student. Thanks to CSX, the grassroots led platform by Harvard and MIT, I was introduced to computer science in 2022, two years after graduating with my Bachelors of Science in Psychology. At that point I was working on my business (which I have now sold to pursue this career). As soon as I learned about coding it was love at first sight. I was more than intrigued by the topic and the world of tech, I knew right then this was going to be my career. From then, I have taken a non-traditional route to break tech. Going back to school to get my Associates in Computer Science, a lot of self teaching, and going the bootcamp route have given me a holistic set of skills!
